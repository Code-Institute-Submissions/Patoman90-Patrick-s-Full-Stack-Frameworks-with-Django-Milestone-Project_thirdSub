About us.

Cyber Sappers Limited was set up by Padriag Boyle and Edward Cazuma. The two started off coding and in a webdevelopment background. In 2019 the two started up the company after both had studied cyber security extensively. Now with Cyber attacks and risks to data on the rise Cyber Sappers or Cyber Engineers has become a security specialist in providing both cyber security hardware,software and security risk assesments based on the clients needs. Cyber Sappers provides both passive and proactive security solutions to their clients. What makes us unique is our services and solutions can be used by large and small clients and everyone inbetween.


Security hardware and Cyber security services

Cyber Sappers have a range of high end hardware solutions that can be added to a network. They are easy to use and easy to install. They provide proactive cyber security barriers to your network security by actively searching and screening all network traffic both incoming and outbound. They use next generation custom software that is updated regularly by their tech team. The range is constantly being updated and we are striving to expand upon small but proven hardware solutions. One of our services includes a customized cyber security software package that our competitors can only sell as a standard package for general network security. Ours meets and exceeds theirs and can be tailored to the needs of the customer.


We also provide our customers a security audit that involves physical security and cyber security audits and testing. We then point out all the risks from locks and electronic security to cyber security. We also have a small team of penetration testers who you can hire for your business to see if they can infiltrate your companies security and reach your server room where you secure your precious data. After we are fininshed you get a feedback on where you can improve the flaws in your system and how we managed to defeat them without detection. All data is then secured on request in our database so if you wish to reference it for 3 months otherwise we will have it securely destroyed to protect your data but the choice is up to you.


Cyber attack repair and maintenance

We offer customers a follow up service or the service alone where we look at your websites,databases,servers or hardware and provide a subscription maintence service that you can arrange to be every 3months - once every 2 years. It is like a health check up but for your cyber security system. We will then point out any outdated or vulnerable elements in the system and advise you on what needs to be done and provide the solutions as required on a case by case basis. Please note that even though we strive to provide the best cyber security solution, like all other providers of cyber security there will still be some chance though slim that a cyber attack can happen. But that will also depend highly on your business.
In the event that you have suffered a cyber attack we are there to help guide and service your security system and do what we can to recover and repair the damage done by any attack from a zero day attack to a trojan. We will then do what we can to upgrade your system and bring it up to modern standards. Again this is done on a case by case basis depending on the clients business needs.
